Frequently Asked Questions

Are OSHA kits available in multiple languages?

Yes, many OSHA-compliant kits now come with multilingual signage, training materials, and documentation to support diverse crews.

What languages are most commonly included in multilingual safety kits?

English and Spanish are the most common, though some kits also include French or visual icons to bridge language gaps.

Do bilingual kits still meet OSHA standards?

Absolutely — as long as required safety information is clearly communicated, multilingual kits can fully comply with OSHA regulations.
